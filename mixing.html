MIXING

Mixing is where dialogue, music, and effects become a single soundtrack that translates across screens and rooms. The goal is clarity, emotional impact, and a cohesive world — without the audience thinking about the mix.

I deliver stereo and 5.1 mixes with dialogue-first intelligibility, balanced music/SFX, and spec-aware delivery for streaming, broadcast, festivals, and theatrical playback.

For the full workflow overview, see Audio Post Production NYC.


When You Need Mixing

  • You’re approaching picture lock and need a final deliverable mix
  • Dialogue needs to feel consistent scene-to-scene
  • Music and effects need to support story without masking dialogue
  • You need predictable deliverables for distribution or festivals

How It Fits Into Post

Mixing is the final stage where dialogue editorial, sound design, ADR, and Foley are shaped into a finished track. Clean editorial upstream makes mixing faster and more flexible — especially when revisions happen late.

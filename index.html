AUDIO POST PRODUCTION IN NYC

Ben Dotson is a New York–based audio engineer specializing in post-production sound for film, television, and long-form narrative. With 10 years of experience across sound design, dialogue editorial, Foley, ADR, dubbing, and re-recording mixing, he helps creators deliver immersive, broadcast-ready soundtracks that support performance and story.

Ben’s credits span features, series, documentaries, and branded content, with work released across major platforms including Netflix, PBS, Amazon Prime, Hallmark, Docubay and Tubi.

Working from a calibrated 7.1 surround studio at Audioworks New York, Ben partners closely with directors, producers, and editors to translate creative intent into detailed, cinematic soundscapes.

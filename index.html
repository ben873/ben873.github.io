ABOUT

Ben Dotson is a New York–based audio engineer specializing in post-production sound for film, television, and long-form narrative. With 10 years of experience across sound design, dialogue editorial, Foley, ADR, dubbing, and re-recording mixing, he helps creators deliver immersive, broadcast-ready soundtracks that support performance and story.

Ben’s credits span features, series, documentaries, and branded content, with work released across major platforms including Netflix, PBS, Amazon Prime, Hallmark, Docubay and Tubi. He is the longtime re-recording mixer for PBS’s GZERO World, delivering consistent, spec-compliant mixes across a high-volume episodic schedule.

Working from a calibrated 7.1 surround studio at Audioworks New York with a streamlined remote workflow, Ben partners closely with directors, producers, and editors to translate creative intent into detailed, cinematic soundscapes. His approach is collaborative and story-first—shaping sound through thoughtful design, Foley, and mix decisions that feel natural, emotional, and precise.

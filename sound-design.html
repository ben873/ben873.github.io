SOUND DESIGN

Sound design builds the world around the picture — environments, motion, transitions, impact, and detail that make scenes feel real. The best sound design supports story and performance without calling attention to itself.

Work may include cinematic environments, vehicles, transitions, and grounded detail that supports editorial intent and strengthens emotional beats.

Full workflow overview: Audio Post Production NYC.


When You Need Sound Design

  • You want the world to feel larger and more immersive than production sound alone
  • Scenes need transitions, motion, or impact that picture suggests but didn’t capture
  • Documentary or archival scenes need support and continuity
  • You want a cohesive sonic identity across the entire project

How It Fits Into Post

Sound design works alongside dialogue editorial, ADR, and Foley, then is shaped into the final soundtrack during the mix. Good sound design also makes mixing more efficient by organizing intent and perspective early.

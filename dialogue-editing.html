DIALOGUE EDITING & CLEANUP

Dialogue editing focuses on making speech clear, consistent, and natural — without drawing attention to the process. It’s the foundation of a professional soundtrack and often determines whether a project feels polished or distracting.

Work typically includes noise reduction, EQ, de-rustle, de-click, and scene matching while preserving performance and tone. The goal is transparency: it should still feel like production sound, just controlled and mix-ready.

Full workflow overview: Audio Post Production NYC.


When You Need Dialogue Cleanup

  • Dialogue was recorded in uncontrolled or noisy environments
  • Multiple microphones (lav/boom) vary noticeably between shots
  • Scenes were shot across different days/locations and don’t match
  • The project is documentary or vérité with limited ADR options

How It Fits Into Post

Dialogue editorial sets up the entire mix. Clean, well-prepared dialogue lets sound design, ADR, Foley, and the final mix move faster — and stay flexible when picture changes occur.

ONSITE MULTITRACK RECORDING

Onsite multitrack recording for music, dance, and staged performance. Also available for select production sound applications that require complex multi-mic setups, live mix support, and individual headphone mixes.

This service is best suited to productions where audio needs go beyond a lightweight bag rig — such as ensemble performance, multiple performers on mic at once, video-driven live events, or situations where a clean live feed and isolated tracks are both important.


When This Is a Good Fit

  • Concerts, recitals, dance performances, and staged events
  • Filmed performances that need a polished live program feed
  • Multi-mic setups where isolated tracks are needed for post-production flexibility
  • Productions that need individual headphone mixes for performers, presenters, or playback support

What the Workflow Supports

Depending on the needs of the project, the workflow may include multitrack recording, live program mixing, basic monitoring support, and coordinated delivery for editorial or video teams.

The goal is to capture a usable live result while preserving the flexibility to repair balances, replace problem mics, or refine the final soundtrack later in post.
